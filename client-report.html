Client Report

How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?

I looked up some pictures of the Tampa, Florida skyline to be used as the hero images. I also used the map of my mission as an image for one of the pages, which was provided in my mission packet.

How did you communicate with your developer? Was there any issues with communication?

I communicated with my developer via email. At the beginning of the porject, the images of my wireframe were blurry and hard to read. This prevented a problem to my developers and they reached out to me asking for clearer pictures. After I provided them, they did not have any further issues with the creation of my website.

Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?

My developer team did not really meet my expectations for the site. The images didn't come up correctly and it isn't very visually appealing. However, all of the content I asked for is present on the pages, which is the most important part.

What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?

I will have a more thought-out idea of what I want for my page. I will also be more in touch with them as they work on my website and be more clear about what I want.

Lead Developer Report

How did you manage your development team and comunicate the tasks for your junior devs?

I split up the tasks of my development team by page. I made the home page and my junior devs made the other two pages for the website.

How did you communicate with your client? Was there any issues?

There were a few communication issues with my client because he was unable to work on the website for the first two thirds of the project time. However, he had already given most of the information needed for the website in the site plan document. As a result, we had everything we needed except for the wireframe, which we made ourselves.

How did you handle publishing your client's site and sharing it with the client?

All information for the page was on GitHub. The first thing I did for the project was make a directory for all of the images and pages in the GitHub repository. Everything that me and my junior devs added to the site was pushed to the client's GitHub page.

How was your experience with managing your site with GitHub?

Managing the site with GitHub made the project easier because everything we needed was in one place. Also, as everyone pushed their updates on the website to GitHub, we had live updates on the HTML and CSS of the website.

Junior Developer Report

How did you communicate with your team lead? Was there any issues?

I communicated with my team leads via email and text. There was an issue with one of my team leads because he was unavailable for the first two thirds of the time we had to work on the project. As a result, the other junior dev and I split up work on two of the given pages instead of doing all three.

Did you know what was expected of you for each site?

The site plans given by the clients were clear in what was expected of us. I followed the provided wireframes and added the content given to me by the client.

Were you able to contribute to your sites?

Yes, I contributed one page for team I was in.

Did you run into any issues with GitHub and adding your changes to the sites?

I didn't run into any issues with GitHub because everyone in my teams pushed their changes to their pages as soon as they could.

General Experience and Reflections

What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

The most challenging part of this project was trying to comminucate with four different teams and working on four different projects simultaneously. It was really stressful because not everyone responded quickly and one of my team members who was in two of my groups was unable to contribute to the project for the first two thirds of the time we were given for the project. I overcame this by communicating with my teammates in class when possible. I also had to make my own wireframe for one of my client's pages because he didn't make any.

What did you enjoy most about this project?

The thing I enjoyed most about this project was making the HTML structure for the pages. I have made a lot of websites during this semester and I was able to copy and paste a lot of my work into the websites for this project. For example, I used an HTML list I made for a previous project in the World Cup 2026 home page.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

i would reach out to all of my teammates before doing any coding for the websites. During this project, the first thing I did was assign pages to my junior devs in my lead dev responsibility. I then started working on the home page for that website instead of reaching out to my other teams, which put me behind schedule for my other websites.

Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.

I do feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this project because I have more experience working with in teams. I was able to see how functional and disfunctional teams work in high-stress situations. After seeing how the functional teams I was on handled the situation and got work done, I can apply the strategies used to make future teams work better.
