Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to entice people to pay us to help them go white water rafting by making it look really appealing. We need to advertise our experience and positive customer reviews, as well as the quality of our supplies and value of our excursions. This will benefit our company because it will encourage more people to become our customers.

Audience

We will be appealing to anyone who is interested in going white water rafting. We will advertise group excursoins with varying degrees of difficulty. That way, if they have little to no experience in white water rafting, they can still sign up and be comfterable and have fun with the experience. If they have a lot of experience and want a challenge, they can sign up for that.
